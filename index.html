NeurIPS 2021 Ghent meetup

December 8-10, 2021 in Ghent, Belgium

With global travel restrictions and lockdown measures in the past year, academic conferences were forced to move to a virtual format. Unfortunately, the NeurIPS 2021 conference is no exception. To provide an opportunity for in person interaction and networking, we are organizing a NeurIPS meetup in Ghent, Belgium. As an official part of NeurIPS 2021, the NeurIPS Ghent meetup will bring the best of the NeurIPS 2021 conference right to Ghent! We will provide a combination of selected NeurIPS content, live talks from domain experts and poster sessions. In addition to the technical content, there will be ample opportunities for social interaction and networking with your peers.

Venue

The NeurIPS Ghent meetup will take place in the historical St Peter's Abbey, right in the city center. This Benedictine abbey was founded in the 7th century by St Amand, who christened the very city of Ghent itself. In the 14th and 15th centuries, it grew into nothing short of an abbey village, with farms, gardens, homes and estates. The abbey owed its prosperity to the privileges it had gained and the taxes it was allowed to impose on its estates. The abbey is now used as a museum and exhibition centre. With its enchanting corridors and romantic courtyard, it is an inspiring location for the NeurIPS 2021 meetup.

The venue is located right in the city center with hotels, restaurants and public transport all within walking distance. There is an underground parking available next to the venue. Please note that the city center is a low emission zone. Check this page first to make sure your car can enter.

Registration

To make sure that we can accommodate everyone in a safe and comfortable way, the NeurIPS Ghent meetup is an invitation-only event. Would you like to attend but have you not received an invitation yet, please contact Sam Leroux and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

Health and safety

Unfortunately, the COVID19 pandemic is still far from over. We are monitoring the situation closely and will follow all local health and safety guidelines. We are committed to providing a safe environment for all attendees. The latest COVID19 regulations (November 26) allow us to proceed with the meetup. These regulations are not expected to change until after the meetup but if you make a hotel or transportation reservation, it is probably a good idea to make sure it has a favorable cancellation policy as the situation can change rapidly. We are required to check your COVID vaccination certificate prior to entering the building. You are also required to wear a mask at all times. Restaurants and bars are open until 11pm and also require a COVID vaccination certificate to enter. At most six people can sit at a single table in restaurants or bars. Shops and museums are open as usual. You can find more information on this page.
